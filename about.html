About Us

HERAD Intercontinental Services Ltd is a proudly Ugandan company transforming lives and uplifting communities through sustainable, diverse business solutions. We are committed to strengthening local industries, empowering entrepreneurs, and connecting Uganda to global markets.
Our business spans Fresh Fruit & Vegetable Exports, Beverages, SME Development, Hospitality, and Tour & Travel. Everything we do is guided by a commitment to impact, sustainability, and shared prosperity.

Our Vision

To build a prosperous and globally competitive Uganda by empowering local businesses, enhancing livelihoods, and promoting sustainable growth across multiple sectors.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality products and services that elevate Uganda’s position in regional and international markets, while improving lives, creating jobs, and supporting sustainable business growth.

Why We Exist

HERAD’s focus is beyond profit. We are committed to:

  • Creating Jobs
  • Raising Household Incomes
  • Uplifting Ugandan Communities
  • Promoting Uganda on the Global Stage

We believe in sustainable growth that benefits everyone along the value chain, from the farmer to the final consumer.
