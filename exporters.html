We Are Exporters: Connecting Local Excellence to the Global Markets


At Herad, we are expert exporters, bridging the gap between our local growers in Uganda and clients worldwide. Our success is built on a global-to-local strategy that ensures reliability and quality.

Our unique team structure is our competitive advantage. We have an on-the-ground "Grower Side" team in Uganda that manages every step from sourcing to quality control. This local presence gives us full visibility over production. We also have a "Customer Side" team in the United States that provides direct, responsive service to one of our key markets, ensuring customer satisfaction and building strong relationships.

This integrated approach guarantees that we deliver consistent quality and reliable service, making us a dependable partner in the global agricultural trade.
