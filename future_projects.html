Future Projects

At Herad, we are building for the future. As part of our long-term growth strategy, we are expanding our agricultural footprint and diversifying into high-potential agribusiness ventures. We are actively seeking strategic partners and investors who share our vision for sustainable, inclusive, and profitable agriculture in Uganda.

500-Acre Contract Farming Development

We have secured 500 acres of fertile land designated for large-scale contract farming. This land will serve as a central hub for structured, climate-smart production of fruits, vegetables, and export crops. The project will also:

  • Provide guaranteed markets for smallholder out-growers
  • Enable access to shared resources such as irrigation, technical training, and input supply
  • Create jobs and stimulate rural economies
  • Strengthen traceability and quality for export markets

We are currently open to investment and partnership opportunities to support infrastructure development, equipment procurement, and agronomic services.

Piggery Expansion Project – Western Uganda

Herad has also acquired 2 acres of land in Western Uganda, dedicated to the development of a modern piggery farm. The site already includes constructed farmhouses and is ready for the next phase of scaling.
This project offers exciting potential for:

  • Commercial pork production
  • Organic manure and biogas initiatives
  • Integration with crop-livestock systems
  • Training and demonstration for local farmers

We invite investors and agribusiness partners to join us in developing this venture into a model piggery operation serving both local and regional markets.

10 Acres in Rakai – Contract Farming Opportunity

We also have 10 acres of arable land in Rakai available for contract farming partnerships. This land is ideal for horticultural production, seed multiplication, or other targeted agriculture programs.
We are looking for development partners interested in:

  • Joint ventures for high-value crops
  • Youth-led agribusiness incubation
  • Donor-funded pilot programs
  • Innovation in irrigation and smart farming solutions

This initiative is designed to support inclusive agricultural growth while providing returns for all stakeholders involved.
